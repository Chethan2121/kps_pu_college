Facilities

A safe, modern and student-centric campus environment.

KPS PU College provides thoughtfully designed infrastructure and facilities that support focused learning, healthy living and balanced growth.

Separate Hostel Facilities

Comfortable, safe and supervised hostel accommodation with dedicated wardens, nutritious food and structured study hours.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls.
  • 24/7 security and CCTV monitoring (where applicable).
  • Study hall with faculty support during key hours.

Library & Reading Room

A well-stocked library with textbooks, reference material, competitive exam guides and periodicals.

  • Calm reading spaces for focused study.
  • Dedicated reference section for NEET, JEE & CET.
  • Access to journals and curated learning resources.

Science & Computer Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer labs that reinforce theoretical concepts with hands-on learning.

  • Lab sessions aligned with the syllabus.
  • Strict safety and supervision protocols.
  • Exposure to problem-solving through experiments.

Digital Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated and well-lit classrooms with digital teaching aids for engaging sessions.

  • Smart boards / projectors where applicable.
  • Comfortable seating for long study hours.
  • Structured seating to facilitate individual focus.

Sports & Wellness

Physical fitness and recreation through indoor and outdoor sports, events and wellness activities.

  • Encouragement to participate in games and events.
  • Helps maintain a healthy balance between study and recreation.

Safety & Pastoral Care

A safe campus environment backed by vigilant staff, clear processes and counselling support.

  • Structured rules for discipline and conduct.
  • Counselling support for academic and personal challenges.
  • Zero-tolerance policy towards harassment or misconduct.
