Courses

Science streams designed for competitive excellence.

KPS PU College offers focused Science combinations with integrated coaching for NEET, JEE and CET, ensuring that classroom teaching and exam preparation move in sync.

PCMB

PCMB – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics & Biology

Ideal for students aspiring for Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy, Life Sciences and allied health sciences. The program blends strong conceptual learning in core subjects with NEET and CET-oriented preparation.

  • Concept-based teaching with emphasis on NCERT & beyond.
  • Regular diagram practice and answer presentation training.
  • Weekly topic-wise tests and quarterly mock exams.
  • Integrated NEET & CET coaching modules.
  • Doubt-clearing clinics after school hours.
  • Performance tracking with personalised feedback.

Competitive Focus

NEET, CET and other medical entrance exam preparation aligned with board syllabus.

Ideal For

Students targeting MBBS, BDS, B.Pharm, BSc (Life Sciences) and related professional programs.

PCMC

PCMC – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics & Computer Science

Perfect for students aiming for Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, and Technology careers. The curriculum emphasises strong mathematical foundations, logical thinking and programming skills.

  • Concept clarity in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
  • Structured JEE / CET oriented practice.
  • Regular coding practice and lab sessions.
  • Focus on algorithmic thinking and logical reasoning.
  • Industry exposure through talks & workshops (where applicable).
  • Career guidance for Engineering and CS programs.

Competitive Focus

JEE Main, CET and other engineering entrance examination readiness.

Ideal For

Students aspiring for B.E/B.Tech, BCA, BSc (CS), Data Science, and related programs.
